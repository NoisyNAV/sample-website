About Route90 Trucking Inc.

Founded in 2010, Route90 Trucking Inc. has grown to become a trusted name in freight transportation and logistics across Canada. Based in Winnipeg, we started with a small fleet and a big vision: to provide reliable, efficient, and customer-focused trucking services. Today, we operate a modern fleet of trucks, serving businesses of all sizes with tailored logistics solutions.

Our mission is to ensure your goods reach their destination safely and on time, every time. We prioritize reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, offering services like freight transportation, warehousing, and custom logistics. With a dedicated team of professionals and a commitment to innovation, we’re proud to support the supply chain needs of businesses across Canada and into the US.

At Route90, we value integrity, teamwork, and excellence. Whether you’re a small business or a large corporation, we’re here to provide seamless transportation solutions that keep your operations running smoothly. Let us be your partner on the road to success.
